Getting Started with Arduino and ESP32

May 19, 2026 11 min read

Arduino vs ESP32: What's the Difference?

Arduino is great for beginners, while ESP32 offers WiFi and Bluetooth capabilities. Choose based on your project needs.

Setting Up Your First Project

Install the Arduino IDE, connect your board, and start programming. Begin with simple projects like LED blinking before moving to complex applications.

Basic Concepts

Understand digital and analog inputs/outputs, serial communication, and how microcontrollers interact with sensors and actuators.

Building Your First IoT Project

Once comfortable with basics, create an IoT project. Connect sensors, send data to the cloud, and control devices remotely.
